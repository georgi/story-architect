# Theme — The Iron Pass

## Value Conflict

**Control vs. Trust**

Serana believes power is the only shield against the powerful. Aldric believes law is the only thing separating civilization from butchery. Both are right. Both are building the thing that will destroy them.

## Facets

1. **Control** — The belief that power protects the powerless
2. **Trust** — The belief that order requires faith in institutions
3. **Corruption** — What happens when control outlives its justification
4. **Loyalty** — What trust costs when the institution betrays you

## Message (Preliminary)

Control and Trust are the same impulse — the need to make a dangerous world survivable — split into two strategies that each require destroying the other to function. The tragedy is not that one corrupts and the other calcifies, but that both are right about what the other will cost, and neither can see that their own remedy is the disease they diagnose in their opponent.
