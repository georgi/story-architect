# Impulse — The Iron Pass

A mountain fortress controlling the only trade route between the Northern Reaches and the Southern Kingdoms. Winter that lasts eight months. A woman who seized the fortress through the miners' guild after her father was executed for demanding fair wages — now she controls every wagon, every toll, every grain shipment that crosses the pass. A magistrate sent from the capital to investigate why the King's tribute has been short for three years. The fortress mines iron and obsidian — whoever controls the mines controls the North. A tavern called "The Black Vein" carved into the rock beneath the fortress where miners drink and old blood oaths still hold. The pass closes when the snows come — and they're coming early this year. Everyone in the fortress depends on the pass for survival. Close the pass, starve the kingdom.
