### 4. world-building.md
**Sources:** McKee, Truby, Marks, production design references

Content to compile:
- Story World: the total domain of the narrative (rules, boundaries, possibilities)
- Sub-worlds method: each major character inhabits a sub-world that embodies their inner state
- Mise-en-scene as storytelling: how physical space reveals character and theme
- World as pressure: environments that force confrontation
- Power structures visible in architecture, geography, and material culture
- Sensory design: sight, sound, smell, texture, temperature per sub-world

### 6. dialogue-voice.md
