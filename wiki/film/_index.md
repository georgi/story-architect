## Articles

### 1. structure.md
**Sources:** Field (*Screenplay*), Gulino (*Screenwriting: The Sequence Approach*), Marks (*Inside Story*), McKee (*Story*)

Content to compile:
- Three-act structure: not a formula but a diagnostic tool — setup, confrontation, resolution
- Sequence approach (Gulino): 8 sequences as building blocks within the three acts
- Midpoint mechanics: false victory, false defeat, point of no return — and how the midpoint redefines the second half
- Act transitions: what must change at each act break (stakes, knowledge, commitment)
- Opening and closing images: visual bookends that frame the transformation
- Climactic sequence design: convergence of all threads, forced choice, irreversible action
- Runtime discipline: what a 90-minute film can carry vs. a 150-minute film
- Genre-specific structural expectations (thriller, drama, horror, comedy) and when to break them
